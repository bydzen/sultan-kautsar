The 1975, About You

  • There's a refuge I hold, a sanctuary unseen,
  • Where your face blooms bright, a memory evergreen.
  • We weave futures untold, in the chambers of our mind,
  • A solace we craft, while how we met we try to find.
  • Grandiose dreams we chase, with a love both fierce and kind,
  • A symphony for two, where echoing emotions bind.
  • Strings weep a poignant tale, a love story yet untold,
  • A melody of longing, in a heart forever bold.
  • In the hush of this retreat, your laughter fills the air,
  • A warmth that chases shadows, a solace beyond compare.
  • We dance in the fading light, with phantoms of what could be,
  • A bittersweet ballet, a love eternally.
  • Though time may blur the edges, and distance play its part,
  • The essence of your being remains etched upon my heart.
  • A testament to a love, that transcends the hands of fate,
  • A love song whispered softly, in a world that seems to wait.
